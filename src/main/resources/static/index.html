Understanding DUP Score

DUP (Deadline Utilization Percentage) measures how much of the available time you use before starting a task.

For example, if you have 10 days until a deadline and you start on day 8, your DUP is 80%.

Formula:

DUP = (Time Waited ÷ Total Time Available) × 100%

Low DUP (0-40%) Start Early

Excellent time management

Medium DUP (40-70%) Moderate Wait

Acceptable approach

High DUP (70%+) Last Minute

Risk of quality issues
